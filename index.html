Laravel Vouchers

An ecommerce voucher generator. Associate vouchers with one or more eloquent model.

Get Started →

❤ Flexible Redeemer Models

Vouchers may be redeemed by any Eloquent model. Useful for multi-auth or User/Guest architecture. Can also define which instances are allowed or denied access.

📝 Multiple redeems

A voucher instance may be set to be redeemed one or more times. Limit scheme may be per instance, associated items or per redeemer.

🔅 Associate one voucher with one or more items

Products may be associated with a voucher to further limit its usage. One voucher may be associated with many items with individual redeem counts.
